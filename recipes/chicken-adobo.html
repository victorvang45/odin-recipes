Chicken Adobo

Description

Chicken Adobo is a Fillipino dish. A delicious tender braised chicken dish that is savory and tangy made with everyday ingredients. A classic Fillipino dish served with rice, and the leftovers still taste great the next day.

Ingredients

  • 4 to 5 pounds chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 3 bay leaves
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Combine the chicken thighs,vinegar,soy sauce, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves in a bowl. Cover and marinate for atleast 3 hours

  2. Transfer the chicken and the marinade into a large pot. Bring the chicken to a boil over high heat. Lower the heat and let it simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  3. Remove the lid after 30 minutes and simmer until the sauce is reduced and thickened for about 20 minutes. Serve over steamed rice.
