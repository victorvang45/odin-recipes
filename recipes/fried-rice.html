Fried Rice

Description

Leftover rice, plus a couple eggs, carrots, peas, and soy sauce is all you need to make fried rice that you could get at your favorite Chinese restraunt. Fried rice is a family-friendly dish that has a lot of flavor. It is loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked white rice, day old rice is best
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce or more
  • 2 tsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients and beat the eggs in a bowl

  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in the pan over medium high heat. Swirl the pan to coat. Pour in eggs and swirl so the eggs form a large think pancake. Lift the edge of the pancake to allow uncooked egg to run to the center. Set the egg aside and cut into 1 inch pieces.

  3. Clean the pan and heat up the remaining oil over high heat. Add the veggies and stir-fry for 1 1/2 minutes. Add the soy sauce, sesame oil, and the rice and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes. Add the eggs set aside eariler and stir until heated through. Serve immediately.
